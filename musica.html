Musica

The Musica app is a unique music player that enables a real-time, shared listening experience through room-based audio playback. Users can create rooms and invite friends to join, allowing everyone to enjoy music together in perfect sync, no matter their location. Built using React Native and Firebase, Musica provides intuitive playback controls, personalized login, and clear track information displays, all within a sleek and user-friendly design. Room hosts manage the playback, ensuring seamless synchronization across devices, making Musica perfect for virtual music sessions and group listening experiences beyond the typical player.
